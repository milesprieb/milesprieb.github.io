Hello, my name is Miles Priebe and I am currently a first year masters student in the Minnesota Robotics Institute (MNRI) M.S. program, at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities. I graduated from the University of Minnesota with a major in Electrical Engineering and a minor in Computer Science. I have internship experience working for as a project engineering intern for Open Systems International (OSI). I also have research experience working as an undergraduate research assistant in the Wearable Technology Lab here at the University of Minnesota. I am currently a graduate research asssistant in the Robotics: Perception and Manipulation Lab. I am passionate about enhancing human-robot interaction through learning-based approaches. I am working on a capstone project tasked with fabricating a soft visuotactile gripper for robust perception and manipulation on a UR5 robot.
